Great authentication needs to carry through your backend and Rownd has your covered with a litenty of backend SDKs and frameworks.  

Steps for getting started:
1. [Set-up](/welcome/getting-started) for more details. your Rownd account (only takes a minute)
2. Create an [App Key and Secret](/configuration/app-credentials)
3. Install Rownd with the SDKs
4. Configure customizations and auth methods from the Rownd platform
