Rownd offers a range of authentication options to maximize both product flexibility and increase user engagement. 

### Navigate to Authentication Methods

Ensure you have the [correct app open](/rownd/guides/configuration/applications). Then click on the overview tab on the left and select Authentication Methods.

![](/images/Screen%20Shot%202022-08-10%20at%2012.22.00%20PM.png)

Finding authentication methods is easy; just click on Overview on the left and select Authentication Methods.

### Summary of Auth methods

[Mobile app sign-in](/rownd/guides/configuration/authentication-methods#mobile-app-sign-in): Radically streamline your mobile download path.

[Email:](/rownd/guides/configuration/authentication-methods/email-sign-in) Passwordless email verification

[Phone Number:](/rownd/guides/configuration/authentication-methods/phone-sign-in) Passwordless SMS based verification

[Sign in with Apple: ](/rownd/guides/configuration/authentication-methods/apple-id-sign-in)Users can authenticate with their Apple IDs

Crypto-wallet: If a crypto wallet provider is recognized, the user will have the option to sign in with a wallet (contact support@rownd.io to enable crypto-wallet auth in your app).
